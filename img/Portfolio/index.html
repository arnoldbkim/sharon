Hello! Welcome to my portfolio page!

My name is Sharon Brook. I am an actress, writer, and Korean translator based in Los Angeles.

I am a fan of whole food plant-based baking and my dream is to play the role of Helen Keller in The Miracle Worker.

I grew up traveling back and forth from the United States and South Korea.

For me, acting is the ability to live in another person's shoes so that we can empathize and connect better with each other as human beings.

And good writing is an art form that lets the audience understand a character's perspective, without necessarily being able to relate to them.

This is what I continue to strive for day by day.
